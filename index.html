I am a tenured associate professor (英才/长聘副教授, 特聘研究员) at the School of Cybersecurity (网络安全学院), College of Intelligence and Computing (智能与计算学部) in Tianjin University (天津大学). I am focusing on Software Security, Mobile Software Security, Open-Source Software Security, AI Security, and Software Analytics (软件安全, 移动软件安全, 开源软件安全, 人工智能安全, 软件分析).
Before that, I was a research assistant professor (科研助理教授) @Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (南洋理工大学) and working in the research group of Prof. Liu Yang (刘杨). I received my Ph.D. degree in East China Normal University (华东师范大学), Shanghai, China (2014-2019), under the superivsion of Prof. Lihua Xu (徐立华) (NYU Shanghai). I had been a research fellow (博士后研究员) (2019-2020) and research assistant (2016-2019) at NTU.
I received two ACM SIGSOFT Distinguished Paper Awards at ICSE 2018 and ICSE 2021.

 Our lab (软件与系统安全课题组，网络空间安全与应用研究团队) has several Ph.D. and Master positions. If you are interested in the research directions below, please send me your CV (senchen@tju.edu.cn). 实验室还有多个博士生和硕士生招生指标，欢迎对软件安全与分析，漏洞挖掘/检测，隐私保护，网络安全感兴趣的同学进行邮件联系! 同时欢迎感兴趣学部（院）校内外研究生推免的同学进行邮件联系！期待你们的加入！
