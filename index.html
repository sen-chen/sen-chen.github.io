I am a full professor at Nankai University and lead NKSSecLab (Nankai Software Security Laboratory). My research focuses on software security and software supply chain security, particularly in terms of vulnerability and malware. I received 6 ACM SIGSOFT Distinguished Paper Awards (ICSE 2018, ICSE 2021, ASE 2022, ICSE 2023, ASE 2023, FSE 2024).

Before that, I was a tenured associate professor and research professor (2021-2024) at Tianjin University, China, a research assistant professor (2020) at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, and worked in the group of Prof. LIU Yang. I was a research fellow (2019-2020) and a research assistant (2016-2019) at NTU.
